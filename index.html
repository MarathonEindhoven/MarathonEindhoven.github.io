--- layout: page title: Eindhoven Marathon Data Collection subtitle: Campaign to collect data for vision based physiology estimation research use-site-title: true ---

Thank you for considering uploading your data for our research. What we would like to have are video recordings of runners in the Marathon (who you have captured on your phone) and athletes can also upload data from their fitness trackers (preferably with heart rate and other available physiology data) such as Strava). This data will be used to investigate the possibility of 'reconstructing' Marathon Eindhoven - recreating and visualising the event using a computer. Moreover, research will be carried out to investigate if its possible to estimate physiological signals from video data.
